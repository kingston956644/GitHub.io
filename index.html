What is a Lumpsum Calculator?

A Lumpsum calculator is a financial tool that helps you estimate the future value of your one-time investment. Whether you are investing in mutual funds, stocks, or fixed deposits, this tool uses the power of compounding to show how your wealth can grow over time.
